Eastside Women of Purpose is putting on their 3rd Annual 4-Person Scramble Charity Golf Tournament on Saturday, May 22nd at The Golf Club at Echo Falls. This year we've added a Banquet & Auction at 12:30pm. Tickets can be purchased separately for the Banquet & Auction.

There will be an 8am Shotgun Start. The Banquet & Auction will begin at 12:30pm.

Join us for 18 holes of golf, gift bags, phenomenal prizes, bragging rights, lunch and an awards ceremony. Sign up as a foursome or we’ll put you on a team.

If you'd like to sponsor the event, please click on Become a Sponsor for more information.

All proceeds to benefit Eastside Women of Purpose.
