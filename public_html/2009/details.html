Eastside Women of Purpose is putting on their 2nd Annual 4-Person Scramble Charity Golf Tournament on Saturday, April 25th at Willows Run in Redmond, WA.

There will be an 8am Shotgun Start. Lunch will start at approximately 12:30pm. Your spouse or significant other is welcome to join you for the afternoon. There is an additional $25 charge.

Join us for 18 holes of golf, gift bags, phenomenal prizes, bragging rights, lunch and an awards ceremony. Sign up as a foursome or we’ll put you on a team.

If you'd like to sponsor the event, please click on Become a Sponsor for more information.

All proceeds to benefit Eastside Women of Purpose and World Concern’s (www.worldconcern.org) San Jai Network educational program.
