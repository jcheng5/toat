Event Details


Directions to Echo Falls

Join us for a morning of golfing, a beautiful lunch, and an afternoon of prizes! The 3rd Annual "Tee Off Against Trafficking" Charity Golf Tournament and Auction will be held on Saturday, May 22, 2010 at The Golf Club at Echo Falls (website, map).

Registration will open at 7:00AM, the tournament will begin with an 8:00AM, and the banquet and auction starts at 12:30PM.

The tournament will be a four-person scramble with a shotgun start. You can sign up as a foursome, or we can put you on a team.

This year, non-golfers are welcome to join us for the banquet and auction for a reduced ticket price.

Golf, Banquet, and Auction ($200/person):

  • Early Bird Registration: Sign up by April 30th and the fee is only $150/person
  • 18 holes of golf at The Golf Club at Echo Falls
  • Drive up valet bag drop
  • Golf cart & score card provided
  • Gift bag including sponsor's products & coupons
  • Phenomenal prizes
  • Recognition & Awards Banquet & Live/Silent Auction
  • The knowledge that you are playing a part in the STOP of human trafficking

Banquet and Auction Only ($50/person):

You don't have to golf to be involved! Join us at 12:30PM for our banquet and live/silent auction.
