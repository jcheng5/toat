Event Details

Join us for a morning of golfing, a beautiful lunch, and an afternoon of prizes! The 3rd Annual "Tee Off Against Trafficking" Charity Golf Tournament and Auction will be held on Saturday, May 22, 2010 at:

The Golf Club at Echo Falls
20414 121st Avenue Southeast
Snohomish, WA
(360) 668-3030
Map/Directions

Registration will open at 7:00AM, the tournament will begin with an 8:00AM shotgun start, and the banquet and auction starts at 12:30PM.

The tournament will be a four-person scramble with a shotgun start. You can sign up as a foursome, or we can put you on a team.

The banquet will include a delicious buffet lunch, silent auction, and high energy live auction (item list). We'll also reveal the tournament results and give out prizes for the winning teams.

This year, non-golfers are welcome to join us for the banquet and auction for a reduced ticket price.
